Some common questions
were often asked

How often should I water my plants?

The watering frequency varies depending on the plant species. We provide detailed watering instructions with each plant, considering factors such as sunlight, humidity, and season.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently, we only offer shipping within India. We are working on expanding our services, so stay tuned for updates.

What if my plant arrives damaged or unhealthy?

We take great care in packaging and shipping our plants. However, if you receive a damaged or unhealthy plant, please contact our customer support within 48 hours with supporting photos, and we will gladly assist you in finding a solution.

Can I return or exchange a plant?

Due to the nature of living plants, we do not accept returns or exchanges. However, if you have any concerns about your purchase, please reach out to our customer support, and we will do our best to address the issue.

How can I care for my plants during extreme weather conditions?

We provide seasonal care tips on our website to help you protect your plants during extreme weather, such as frost, heatwaves, or storms. Additionally, we offer advice on suitable indoor environments for plants during harsh weather conditions.
